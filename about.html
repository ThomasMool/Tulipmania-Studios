Our Story

What began as a shared passion from a young age while growing up in the Dutch flower bulb district has evolved into Tulipmania Studios: an independent filmmaking collective dedicated to crafting distinctive, independent films.

Combining expertise in screenwriting, directing, cinematography, sound, and music, founders Maarten Moolenaar, Noah Enderlé, and Thomas Moolenaar are driven by the thrill of creation and the satisfaction of bringing projects to life alongside a dedicated team.

Through Tulipmania Studios, we aim to tell meaningful stories with authenticity, craftsmanship, and passion. We invite you to follow our creative journey and share in the love of filmmaking that continues to inspire everything we create.
