Our Story

Founded in the heart of the Dutch flower bulb district, Tulipmania studios combines expertise in screenwriting, directing, cinematography, sound, and music.

We, Maarten Moolenaar, Noah Enderlé, and Thomas Moolenaar are driven by creating something new and exciting every time. In that fashion, two short films have been created, and more films are in the pipeline.

Through Tulipmania Studios, we aim to tell meaningful stories with authenticity, craftsmanship, and passion. We invite you to follow our creative journey and share in the love of filmmaking that continues to inspire everything we create.
