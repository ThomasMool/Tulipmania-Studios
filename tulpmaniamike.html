Tulpmania Mike

Summary:

What: Short film
Genre: Adventure / Comedy
Location: Dutch countryside, NL
Language: Dutch
Film length: ca. 8 minutes
Shooting period: March 2025
Cast & crew: 1 cast, 3 crew

Film plot:

In the Dutch countryside, "Tulpmania Mike" has devoted his life to solving a 400-year-old horticultural mystery. While his neighbors see an oddball obsessed with flowers, Mike believes an ancestral riddle will lead him to the most extraordinary tulip ever known. On the long-awaited day of reckoning, his search takes an unexpected turn.
