De Momentenvanger

Summary:

What: Short film
Genre: Psychological Drama
Location: Amsterdam, NL
Language: Dutch
Film length: ca. 15 minutes
Shooting period: July - August 2025
Cast & crew: 5 cast, 3-5 crew

Film plot:

When photographer Philip becomes consumed by preserving life's last fleeting moments, he starts pushing away the people who care about him most. Haunted by loss and guided by the philosophy of a former mentor, he chases an impossible photograph. In his search for the ultimate final moment, Philip risks becoming part of the image himself.
